About Us

Mumbai Eye Care is one roof for various super specialties of eye care such as Cataract, cornea, Lasik, Pediatric Ophthalmology, Oculoplastics, Ocular Aesthetics, Ocular Oncology, Glaucoma, and Retina. We strictly adhere to internationally acclaimed operational procedures to reduce errors and enhance patients safety along with all the statutory & regulatory requirements laid.

Our Vision

As a Leader in eye care and to provide compassionate vision care to all, by combining the most advanced technology with our highly trained staff and to be known as one of the best eye clinics in Ghatkopar, Mumbai.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate people and spread awareness about various eye disorders. We are committed to incorporate latest technology and advancements in the eye care field to provide an overall better eye health.

Our Leadership

Dr. Jatin Ashar, trained at the prestigious 'All India Institute of Medical Sciences', New Delhi; one of Asia's best and did fellowship in Cornea and Anterior Segment from the world renowned L. V. Prasad Eye Institute. His areas of expertise are Lamellar Keratoplasty, Pediatric Keratoplasties and LASIK Surgery. Dr. Jatin doesn't only specializes in cornea transplant surgeries but also trains other doctors from India and abroad in performing cornea transplant surgeries and has a very high success rate of cornea transplant surgery.
